We are improving healthcare by getting healthcare professionals the information about their patients they need when they need it.

We provide a search engine to find people and their search history. That means no more waiting for records to be transferred from other offices or asking someone to remember everything they've been treated for.

We prioritize patient privacy. In fact, we don't even store patient data. Our software is the middle man between you and other offices, hospitals, insurance companies, and anyone else that might have the records you need. We monitor who is using our technology to ensure it is being retrieved in compliance with HIPAA.
